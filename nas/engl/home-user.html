About healthy foods
  • Avocado is very nutritious and contains a wide range of nutrients, including many vitamins and minerals. Only 100 g of avocado contains vitamin K, vitamin C, potassium, vitamin B5, vitamin B6 and vitamin E, as well as small amounts of magnesium, manganese, copper, iron, zinc, phosphorus, vitamins A, B1, B2 and B3. In addition, it contains 160 calories, 2 grams of protein and 15 grams of healthy fats, it does not contain cholesterol and sodium, and it has a very small percentage of saturated fats.
  • Almond are useful and healthy foods, which contain a large number of vitamins, minerals, fiber and amino acids. An ingredient in a salad, a piece of cake, a favorite snack… Yes, almonds are a food that many people often use. In addition to being delicious, almonds are, according to many experts, a very useful and healthy food.
  • Sweet potato is a food that is considered tropical and originates from Central and South America. We find it in white, yellow, orange and red. It is grown en masse in America, Africa and Asia, and lately it is more and more on our continent. Since sweet potatoes are a starchy food, they are used in cooking as well as potatoes, and you won't go wrong if you completely replace potatoes with it.
  • Garlic is a herbaceous plant from twenty to 40cm, with a bulb of strong aroma and spicy taste. The leaves are elongated, flat, narrow, leathery and end in a spike. Everyone participates in the creation of membranes with a membrane skin. The flowers are white or pink on long stalks, grouped in a simple terminal shield, which opens before flowering. The flower is composed of three calyx and three coronal, free and permanent petals. The fruit is three-partitioned with three seams and three lodges, which bear two seeds each. The seeds are angular and hard, black, albumen fleshy.
  • Broccoli contains twice as much vitamin C as oranges. It is a vitamin that helps utilize iron, prevents the appearance of cataracts, relieves cold symptoms and strengthens the body's general defenses. Broccoli also contains B vitamins, especially B3, B5 and folic acid - which supports the growth of fetal tissue.
  • Ginger is at the same time an antioxidant that neutralizes harmful free radicals in the body. Also, ginger reduces the risk of cancer. Recent research has shown that ginger can prevent cells from transitioning from a benign to a malignant state.
